Sahii Paneer

Description

Shahi Paneer is a Mughlai dish where paneer (Indian cottage cheese) is cooked in a creamy gravy made of onions, yogurt, nuts, and seeds.
The dish combines the luxurious texture of paneer with the aromatic flavors of Shahi spices.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Indian Cottage Cheese): 1 ½ cups (cubed)
  • Curd (Plain Yogurt): ½ cup (avoid sour curd)
  • Ginger Garlic Paste: 1 teaspoon
  • Red Chili Powder (optional): ¼ teaspoon
  • Turmeric (optional): 0.12 teaspoon
  • Garam Masala: 1 teaspoon
  • Saffron Strands (optional): A pinch
  • Salt: ½ teaspoon (adjust to taste)
  • Ghee (or oil): 2 tablespoon
  • Kewra Water (optional): 4 drops
  • Water: ¾ cup
  • Cream (optional): 3 tablespoons
  • Cumin (or jeera or shahi jeera, optional): ½ teaspoon
  • Cloves: 3
  • Cinnamon: 2 inches
  • Whole Cashewnuts (split or kaju): 12
  • Almonds (or badam): 8
  • Green Cardamoms (or elaichi): 3
  • Green Chili Slit (optional): 1
  • Onions (cubed, 2 medium, avoid purple onions): 1 cup
  • Tomatoes (cubed, optional, refer notes): 2

Preparation

  • Soak the cashew nuts in hot water for 15-20 minutes.
  • Cut the paneer into your desired shape and soak it in warm water.
  • Whisk the yogurt with water and keep it aside.
  • Combine onion, ginger, garlic, and soaked cashews in a blender jar. Add water and blend to make a smooth puree.
  • Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and sauté until fragrant.
  • Add the onion-cashew puree and cook for 10 minutes.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, green chili, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  • Pour in the whisked yogurt and mix well.
  • Add the paneer cubes and simmer for 2 minutes.
  • Garnish with garam masala and serve hot with naan or roti.
